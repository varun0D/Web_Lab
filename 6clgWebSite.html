JSS Science and Technology University

About the University

JSS Science and Technology University (JSS STU), Mysuru, is one of the leading technical universities in Karnataka. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate and research programs in Engineering, Science and Management.

Departments

  • Computer Science and Engineering
  • Information Science and Engineering
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering

Facilities

  • Digital Library
  • Wi-Fi Campus
  • Modern Laboratories
  • Sports Complex
  • Hostel Facility
